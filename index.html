Our Philosophy

Capital allocated with
macro conviction

"Markets are governed by macro regimes, not random walks."

We believe the global macroeconomic landscape is shaped by identifiable structural forces — central bank policy cycles, capital flow dynamics, and geopolitical regime shifts. Fingran's edge lies in recognising these inflection points before they are priced by consensus.


Our process integrates quantitative signals with deep discretionary judgment, drawing on decades of trading experience across G10 and emerging market currencies.

01
Regime Awareness
We classify macro environments into distinct regimes and size positions accordingly, rather than assuming stationarity.
02
Asymmetric Risk
Every position must offer a favourable risk/reward profile. We cut losers fast and let winners run within macro thesis.
03
Cross-Asset Signal
Currency conviction is validated through rates, commodities, and equity flows — we do not trade FX in isolation.
04
Capital Preservation
Drawdown control is paramount. We manage at portfolio level with strict VaR limits and scenario stress-testing.

Investment Strategies

Three distinct approaches

Each strategy is managed independently with dedicated risk budgets, allowing institutional allocators to access targeted exposures.

Global Macro FX

A discretionary macro strategy expressing views on G10 and select EM currency pairs through spot, forwards, and options. Driven by central bank divergence and current account dynamics.

Flagship · Open
Systematic Carry

A quantitative carry strategy that harvests FX risk premia across a diversified basket of currency pairs, with dynamic hedging overlays during risk-off regimes.

Systematic · Open
EM Macro Select

Opportunistic exposure to high-conviction emerging market macro themes, combining FX, local rates, and sovereign credit instruments in a concentrated, high-conviction book.
